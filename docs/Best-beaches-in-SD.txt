# Favorite Beaches in San Diego
Source: Reddit r/sandiego (https://www.reddit.com/r/sandiego/comments/osxcqd/whats_your_favorite_beach_in_san_diego/)
Topic: Users discussing their favorite beaches in San Diego.

## Top Beach Recommendations & User Comments:

### 1. Coronado Beach
- **Highlights:** Glimmering sand, wide beach with enough space so it never feels too crowded. Great for a "Victorian beach vibe."
- **Notes:** Consistently listed as a top favorite by multiple users.

### 2. Black's Beach (Specifically South Black's)
- **Highlights:** Very secluded with high sheer cliffs in the back. Gives off a "secret beach" feel.
- **Notes:** It is a well-known nude beach. Users recommend the southern part (between Ho Chi Minh and the lifeguard station) for maximum seclusion, avoiding the more crowded area right below the gliderport. The hike down is steep but "well worth the solitude." 

### 3. Windansea Beach
- **Highlights:** Beautiful surf spot and an excellent location for sunsets. 

### 4. Torrey Pines State Beach
- **Highlights:** Beautiful backdrop and great hiking. 
- **Parking/Facilities:** Has a paywall (state parks pass works) which helps keep the area pristine. Bathrooms are fantastic. Pro-tip: Park for free on Carmel Valley Road and walk in.
- **Drawbacks:** One user noted it can be rocky with wet sand up to the cliffs.

### 5. Moonlight Beach (Encinitas)
- **Highlights:** Well maintained with nice cliffs in the back. Described by users as "by far the cleanest and best for bathing."

### 6. La Jolla (Marine Room to La Jolla Cove / Shores)
- **Highlights:** Best for snorkeling and encountering wildlife. The water visibility is great for spotting leopard sharks (often seen right in front of the Marine Room straight out).
- **Notes:** Clean and receives special attention, though can get crowded.

### 7. Ocean Beach (OB) & Dog Beach
- **Highlights:** Loved for its neighborhood vibe. Dog Beach is specifically praised for pet owners.
- **Notes:** Sparks debate; some love the "family side," while others find it less clean compared to other spots.

### 8. Pacific Beach (PB) & Mission Beach
- **Highlights:** Fun environment with a great boardwalk. 

### 9. Cardiff State Beach
- **Highlights:** Easy parking, clean bathrooms, cool walk along the cliffs, dog-friendly.
- **Pro-tip:** Grab food and drink at Seaside Market before heading down.

### 10. Imperial Beach (IB)
- **Highlights:** "The People's Beach." Easy-going vibe, very friendly, and recent revamps have made it a nice spot. Good for lobstering and fishing.

### 11. Silver Strand State Beach
- **Highlights:** Very family-friendly.
- **Parking:** State pay parking lot means there is almost always easy parking. Great for a quiet dip or reading a book.

### 12. Del Mar (17th Street)
- **Highlights:** Features a Snack Shack and showers right on site. Good waves.

### 13. Other Mentions:
- **No Surf:** Small, quaint, and hard to get to.
- **Tourmaline:** Highly polarizing; some love it, while others jokingly (or seriously) say "it sucks, avoid at all costs" to keep crowds away.
- **Sunset Cliffs:** Great for sunsets and taking dogs.
- **D-Street in Encinitas** & **Fletchers Cove in Solana Beach.**
- **Breakers Beach:** Located on North Island near Coronado.
